Profitru platform overview for Indian ecommerce teams

Profitru is a profit and operations workspace for sellers who list on Amazon, Flipkart, other marketplaces, or their own store. This article is the map: how each capability connects to what you see on the homepage and to the deeper posts in this series. Profitru uses bulk uploads, not live marketplace APIs, so you can start without engineering projects.

The problem Profitru solves

Finance sees payouts, ops sees WMS rows, growth sees ad dashboards—and none of them share one SKU-level truth. That gap shows up as “profitable” SKUs that are actually bleeding once returns, GST, and dead stock are included.

Profitru is built to be the single command centre Indian teams describe on the marketing site: structured data in, reconciled views out.

  • SKU-level margin after fees, ads, returns, and tax treatment
  • Inventory that reflects purchases, dispatch, and returns
  • Procurement hints tied to velocity and profitability
  • Returns and QC that update stock and margin honestly
  • Dispatch visibility so channels and warehouse agree
  • Claim tracking next to operational events
  • Exports for finance and auditors
  • Role-appropriate access for warehouse and HQ
  • AI Profit Assistant on Pro for early warnings

How data gets in

You export reports from marketplaces and tools you already use, then upload them in bulk. Profitru normalises and links those files so the same SKU and channel identifiers flow through profit, inventory, and operations views—see platform exports & CSV for the practical workflow.
