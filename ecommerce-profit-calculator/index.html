Ecommerce profit calculator: D2C net margin beyond Shopify revenue

An ecommerce profit calculator worth the name captures every rupee that leaves before you keep margin: acquisition, payment rails, cash-on-delivery leakage, returns, packaging, and the inventory you financed but never sold. Top-line GMV from your storefront is not profit.

Acquisition and contribution margin

Paid social, search, and influencer spend must attach to cohorts and SKUs, not a single blended CAC. Contribution margin after variable marketing tells you which campaigns fund themselves—and which only look good in platform dashboards.

Payment stack and COD reality

Gateway fees, EMI subsidies, RTO on COD, and reconciliation delays change effective price. Indian ecommerce still carries meaningful COD share; your calculator should model return-to-origin as a first-class line item, not an afterthought in finance.

Fulfilment and returns

  • First-mile and last-mile costs per order, including split shipments.
  • Returns and refurb: restocking fees, quality loss, and write-offs.
  • Inventory risk: MOQ buys versus sell-through on owned channels.

Where Profitru fits
